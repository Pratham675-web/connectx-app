✓ Message Tick System — How it works
✓
Single Tick ● Grey
Message has been sent from your device.
Recipient's internet is OFF — they are not connected to the server. Message is waiting to be delivered.
✓✓
Double Tick ● Black
Message has been delivered.
Recipient is online and connected — but they are currently in a different chat or on another screen. They haven't opened your conversation yet.
✓✓
Double Tick ● Blue
Message has been seen!
Recipient is online and is currently in this chat — reading your messages right now! 👀
🏆 Loyalty Score System
Category 1 Online but Not Seen
When User B is online but didn't open your message —
Points added = ½ × delay (in minutes)
Example: Sent at 7:25 AM, replied at 10:00 AM = 145 mins → +72 points
⚠️ Very Dangerous Seen but No Reply (>5 min)
Message was seen but reply came after more than 5 minutes —
Points added = Full delay (in minutes)
Example: Seen at 7:25, replied at 8:00 = 35 mins → +35 points
Category 3 Seen but Late Reply (≤5 min)
Message was seen and replied within 5 minutes —
Points added = ½ × delay (in seconds) ÷ 60
Example: 2 min delay = 120 sec → 60 sec → +30 points
✅ What does your score mean?
Score is out of 800 points
• Below 200 → You are 98% Loyal according to ConnectX 🎉
• 200–500 → Moderate loyalty — sometimes slow to respond
• Above 500 → Low loyalty — people are waiting too long!
